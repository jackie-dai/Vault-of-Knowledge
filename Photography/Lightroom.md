Exposure
- Controls overall brightness of image 
- lets more light into the image 

Contrast
- this manipulates the black and white
- the same effect can be achieved by adjusting the white and black

Highlights
- The bright spot, similar to whites, but has color

Shadows
- Darkens and brightens the blacks

Whites
- Literally the white in pixels in the photo (pure white)
- related to highlights

Black
- Literally the black pixels in the photo (pure black)
- related to shadows
