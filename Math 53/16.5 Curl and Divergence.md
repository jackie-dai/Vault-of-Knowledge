
Finding the Curl
$$
F = Pi + Qj + Rk
$$
$$
curl(F) = (\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial R}{\partial z})i + (\frac{\partial P}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial R}{\partial z})j + (\frac{\partial P}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y})k
$$

A vector field is conservative if
$$
curl(F) = 0
