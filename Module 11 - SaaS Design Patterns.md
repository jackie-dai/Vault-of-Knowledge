
## Unified Modeling Language (UML)
A graphical notation system for diagramming the architecture of an application. It visualizes the associations between classes along with the attributes and methods of each class.

![[Pasted image 20251218114343.png]]
Notation
- Association (holds a reference to)
	- 0..* = "zero or more"
	- 1 = "exactly one"
	- 1..* = "one or more"
- White diamond (aggregation)
	- Weak ownership
- Black diamond (composition)
	- Strong ownership
