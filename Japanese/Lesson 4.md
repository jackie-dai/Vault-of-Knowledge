## Object marker を
Verbs require a subject and object, we mark the subject with は or が and mark objects with を.


*を becomes は in negative statements*

# Time marker に
used for marking a point in time

*Note: relative time expression (e.g. あした、きょう) don't use に, with the exception being optional for しゅうまつ*

*Rule of thumb: you can't use を twice in a sentence*
