
## 時に 
Used for connecting two clauses. Same meaning as "when" 
日本へ行く時に、まｔちゃをのみます。
When I go to japan, I will drink matcha

---
## Verbal Noun + に行く

冬休みに日本へhomestay行くつもりです。

## Deriving Adverbs from Adjectives
*An adverb is an adjective that modifies a verb (ex. beautiful -> beautifully)*

い　～　く
大きいー＞大きく

な　～　に
きれいー＞きれいに
