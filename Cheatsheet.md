
## Central Limit Theorem
Within 1 SD of the mean is 65%
Within 2 SD is 95%

## Beta
Prior: Beta(r, s)
Posterior: Beta(r+h, n-h+s)

To find the density curve, find the mode (peak of the curve)
$$
Mode=\frac{r-1}{r+s-2}
$$
