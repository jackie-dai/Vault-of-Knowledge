

## Posterior
The posterior is equal to the **Prior** x **likelihood**

**Prior**
$$
X-Beta(r, s)
$$
**Posterior**
$$
X-Beta(r+h, n-h+s)
$$

## Map Estimate

$$
MAP = \frac{r-1}{r+s-2}
$$

## Conjugate Priors
If the prior distribution is a beta density and the posterior is also a beta density, then the beta family is a family of conjugate priors for the binomial distribution
